01 — About

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at Yonsei University (QS #50), Seoul, South Korea, working in the Department of Industrial Engineering under Prof. Biswajit Sarkar. My research sits at the intersection of Operations Research and sustainability — designing mathematical models and multi-objective optimization frameworks for smart supply chains, green energy systems, and smart factories.

I completed my Ph.D. in Mathematics (Operations Research) at Graphic Era Deemed University, India, funded by the prestigious DST-INSPIRE Fellowship of the Government of India. My doctoral research focused on sustainable biofuel supply chain management through advanced mathematical modelling.

With over 14 peer-reviewed publications in leading SCIE/Q1 journals published by Elsevier, Wiley, and SpringerNature, I aim to contribute data-driven, analytically rigorous solutions to real-world sustainability challenges — from energy supply chains to waste management and smart manufacturing.
